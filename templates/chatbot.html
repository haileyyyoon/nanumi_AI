House of Sharing Chatbot

This archive answers questions using testimony, historical records, and research gathered by the House of Sharing (나눔의 집) and related sources documenting Japan’s wartime military sexual slavery system. Answers are drawn strictly from the archive — if something isn’t in the record, the assistant will say so.
